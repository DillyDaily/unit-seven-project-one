Upcycling an old Vanity into a Kitchen Island

It's not only hip to be green, it'll also save you a ton of money!

Materials for this project will depend on the state of the vanity, but here is a short list of items you should expect:

✔Wood planks for the top

✔Large MDF panel for base

✔Paint

✔4-8 casters

Only a few steps to build out your new kitchen island:

  1. Secure the MDF panel to the base of the vanity
  2. Attach the casters to the bottom of the MDF panel
  3. Anchor the top of the island to the vanity
  4. Finish the top & paint the base

If you are interested in upcycling old furniture, I highly recommend checking out the following stores:

  • StardustBuilding.org
  • HabitatRestores.org
  • PorterBarnwood.com
